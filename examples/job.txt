Frontend / Full-Stack Engineer

Company: Horizon Workflow
Location: Remote
Seniority: Senior

Horizon Workflow is building collaboration tools for operations teams. We are looking for a senior frontend or full-stack engineer who can own user-facing product work while collaborating closely with product, design, and backend engineers.

Responsibilities:
- Build polished, reliable product experiences with React and TypeScript.
- Design and consume APIs for workflow, reporting, and account-management features.
- Improve application performance and maintainability.
- Write automated tests for critical user journeys.
- Work with product managers and designers to turn ambiguous requirements into shipped features.
- Mentor other engineers through code reviews and technical planning.

Required skills:
- Strong TypeScript and React experience.
- Experience with Node.js or another backend runtime.
- API integration experience with REST or GraphQL.
- Testing experience with Jest, Playwright, Cypress, or similar.
- Strong communication and product collaboration.

Nice to have:
- PostgreSQL experience.
- Accessibility knowledge.
- Experience with analytics dashboards or B2B SaaS products.
- CI/CD experience.
